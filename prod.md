# MyLiverApp - Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

## 1. Vision

To create a user-friendly mobile application that empowers individuals with liver diseases to take control of their health by simplifying the tracking of key nutritional data and medications.

## 2. Target Audience

The primary users are patients diagnosed with various liver conditions (e.g., Fatty Liver Disease, Cirrhosis, Hepatitis) who need to adhere to strict dietary and medication regimens.

## 3. Core MVP Features

### 3.1. User Onboarding & Profile

- **Initial Setup:** A simple, guided process for new users to set up their profile.
- **Health Profile:** Input basic health information, including type of liver condition and dietary restrictions (e.g., low sodium, fluid restriction).
- **Goal Setting:** Allow users or their healthcare providers to set daily/weekly nutritional goals.

### 3.2. Nutrient Tracking

- **Macro Tracking:** Simple input for tracking daily intake of protein, carbohydrates, and fats.
- **Micro Tracking:** Focused tracking of critical micronutrients for liver health, primarily **Sodium** and **Potassium**.
- **Fluid Intake:** A dedicated section to log daily water and fluid consumption.
- **Manual Entry:** For the MVP, all food and nutrient data will be entered manually. A simple form will be used to add meals and their nutritional information.

### 3.3. Medication Management

- **Medication Log:** A list where users can add all their prescribed medications, including dosage and frequency.
- **Daily Tracking:** A simple checklist or logging mechanism for users to mark when they have taken their medications.
- **Reminders:** Basic push notifications to remind users when it's time to take a scheduled medication.

### 3.4. Dashboard / Home Screen

- **Daily Summary:** A clear, at-a-glance view of the current day's tracked nutrients against their set goals (e.g., "Sodium: 1200mg / 2000mg").
- **Medication Status:** A visual indicator showing which medications have been taken for the day.
- **Quick Add Buttons:** Fast access to log a meal, fluid, or medication.

## 4. Technology Stack

- **Framework:** React Native with Expo
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** Nativewind (Tailwind CSS for React Native)
- **UI Components:** shadcn/ui or similar pre-built components.

## 5. Success Metrics for MVP

- **User Adherence:** Percentage of active users who log data at least 4 days a week.
- **Complete Profiles:** High rate of users completing the initial onboarding and setting up their nutritional goals and medication lists.
- **Qualitative Feedback:** Positive feedback from initial users regarding the app's ease of use and its helpfulness in managing their condition.
